About Bingerz

Bingerz is a movie browsing website. We strive to give you the best experience while looking for your favourite movies. All the data is fetched live from the TMDB public database and laid out in a comprehensive manner.


Why Bingerz ?

To get the most trusted data from the biggest database in the industry. Even the posters don't get stale. They get updated frequently.

We provide detailed yet crisp overview without having to load multiple web pages adding to the ease of usability.
